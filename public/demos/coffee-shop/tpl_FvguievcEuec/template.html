Our Story

Born from a passion for exceptional coffee and genuine community connection

Founded in 2018 by coffee enthusiasts Maria and James, Roasted Roots began as a dream to create more than just another coffee shop. We envisioned a space where neighbors become friends, where every cup tells a story, and where the aroma of freshly roasted beans brings people together.

Our beans are sourced directly from small-scale farmers who share our commitment to sustainable practices and exceptional quality. Every batch is roasted in-house using traditional methods that honor the bean's natural flavors while creating the perfect balance our community has come to love.

Today, we're proud to serve as the heart of our neighborhood, supporting local artisans, hosting community events, and brewing memories one cup at a time.
